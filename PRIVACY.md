# MatchMaxxer Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 3, 2026_

MatchMaxxer ("the app") is a single-player iOS game. We've designed it to collect as little information as possible. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it goes, and what choices you have.

## Who we are

MatchMaxxer is developed by Casey Millstein ("we", "us"). You can reach us at **henry.langmack@gmail.com** for any privacy questions or requests.

## Data the app stores on your device

The app stores the following on your device only, using Apple's standard `UserDefaults`:

- The **initials** you choose for the leaderboard (up to 3 characters).
- A local **history of your recent scores** (most recent 50 rounds), including score, difficulty, category, and timestamp.

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to submit a score to Game Center (see below). Deleting the app removes it.

## Data handled by Apple on our behalf

The app uses standard Apple services. When you opt in to these features, Apple processes data under its own privacy policy:

- **Game Center (optional).** If you sign in to Game Center and submit a score, Apple receives your score and your Game Center alias so it can display you on the global leaderboard. We do not operate this leaderboard — it is hosted by Apple. See Apple's Game Center privacy information at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.
- **In‑App Purchases.** The app offers a one‑time, non‑consumable Sound unlock via Apple's StoreKit. Payment, receipts, and "Restore Purchase" are handled entirely by Apple. We do not see your payment details.

## Data we do NOT collect

- We do not run our own servers, accounts, or backend.
- We do not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.
- We do not use third‑party SDKs for tracking or advertising.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites (`NSPrivacyTracking = false`).
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Permissions

The app does not request access to the microphone, camera, location, photos, contacts, or notifications. If a future version adds a permission, we will update this policy and the App Store privacy labels first.

## Your choices

- **Initials and local history** can be reset by deleting the app.
- **Game Center participation** is optional; you can sign out at any time in iOS Settings → Game Center.
- **Purchases** are managed in your Apple ID account. Refunds go through Apple at https://reportaproblem.apple.com.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, surface the change in the app or on the App Store listing.
